Noodles with Omelette

Servings: 1-2

Ingredients

For the Noodles:

  • 1 packet of instant noodles (any flavor)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup mixed vegetables (e.g., carrots, bell peppers, and peas)
  • 1/4 teaspoon soy sauce (optional for extra flavor)

For the Omelette:

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon chopped onions
  • 1 tablespoon chopped bell peppers (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Method

  1. Prepare the Noodles:
    • Boil water in a pot and add the instant noodles along with the flavoring packet.
    • Add the mixed vegetables and let it cook according to the instructions on the noodle packet (usually 3-5 minutes).
    • For additional flavor, add a splash of soy sauce. Stir well and set aside.
  2. Prepare the Omelette:
    • In a small bowl, crack the eggs and whisk with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Add chopped onions and bell peppers (if using) and mix well.
    • Heat oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat, pour in the egg mixture, and spread evenly in the pan.
    • Cook for 2-3 minutes on one side, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until fully set.
    • Once cooked, remove from the pan and cut into strips or squares.
  3. Serve: Place the cooked noodles on a plate and top with the omelette slices. Serve hot.
