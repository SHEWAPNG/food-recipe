Egusi Soup

Ingredients:

  • Egusi seeds
  • Fish
  • Locust beans
  • Onions
  • Palm oil

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the egusi by grinding it into a fine powder.
  2. Fry the onions in palm oil until golden brown.
  3. Add locust beans and stir for a minute.
  4. Add the ground egusi and water, then stir until well combined.
  5. Add fish and let it cook for a few minutes.

Fish Stew

Ingredients:

  • Fish
  • Palm oil
  • Maggi seasoning
  • Salt

Instructions:

  1. Heat palm oil in a pot.
  2. Add onions and fry until translucent.
  3. Add seasoning and salt to taste.
  4. Fry fish separately if desired, then add to the stew.
  5. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
