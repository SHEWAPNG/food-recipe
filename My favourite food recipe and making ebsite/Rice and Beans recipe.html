Rice and Beans with Fish Stew

Ingredients

For Rice and Beans:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 cup beans (black-eyed or kidney beans)
  • Salt to taste

For Fish Stew:

  • 2 pieces of fish (e.g., tilapia or catfish)
  • 1/2 cup palm oil
  • 2 tomatoes, blended
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • Salt and seasoning to taste

Method

  1. Prepare the Rice and Beans: Cook beans until tender, then add rice, salt, and cook until both are soft.
  2. Make the Fish Stew: Fry onions in palm oil, add blended tomatoes, and cook until thickened. Add fish and season, cooking until fish is tender.
  3. Serve: Serve rice and beans with fish stew.
