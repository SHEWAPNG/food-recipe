Rice and Fried Stew

Ingredients for Rice

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 4 cups of water
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients for Fried Stew

  • 6 large tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1-2 Scotch bonnets (to taste)
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 cup palm or vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon ground crayfish (optional)
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Cooked chicken, beef, or fish (optional)

Preparation

  1. Cooking the Rice: Rinse the rice in water until clear. Add 4 cups of water, a pinch of salt, and bring to a boil. Cook until soft, drain if necessary, and set aside.
  2. Preparing the Fried Stew: Blend the tomatoes, bell peppers, Scotch bonnets, and onion into a smooth mixture.
  3. In a pot, heat oil over medium heat. Pour in the blended mixture and fry for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally to avoid burning.
  4. Add seasoning cubes, ground crayfish (optional), and salt to taste. Stir well.
  5. Continue frying until the oil separates from the sauce, indicating itâ€™s well-cooked.
  6. Add cooked chicken, beef, or fish if desired, and let it simmer in the stew for another 5 minutes.
  7. Serve the fried stew over cooked rice, and enjoy!
