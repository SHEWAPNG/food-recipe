Orange

Nutritional Facts

Calories: 62
Vitamin C: 92% of RDI
Fiber: 3g
Folate: 9% of RDI
Potassium: 237 mg

How to Eat

Oranges are eaten fresh by peeling and segmenting. They can be juiced or added to salads. Their zest is used in cooking to enhance flavor.

Washing Tips

Wash oranges under running water and scrub the skin if the zest will be used in cooking.
