Mango

Nutritional Facts

Calories: 99
Vitamin C: 67% of RDI
Fiber: 2.6g
Vitamin A: 10% of RDI
Folate: 18% of RDI

How to Eat

Mangoes are eaten fresh, cubed, or blended into smoothies. They are also used in salsas, salads, and desserts.

Washing Tips

Wash mangoes under running water, scrubbing gently with hands before drying and cutting.
