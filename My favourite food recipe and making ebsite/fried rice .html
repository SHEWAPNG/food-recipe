Fried Rice with Chicken or Beef

Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

For the Fried Rice:

  • 3 cups rice (preferably long-grain)
  • 4-5 cups chicken or beef broth
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1 cup diced green beans
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn
  • 1/2 cup diced bell peppers (any color)
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2-3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Fried Chicken or Beef:

  • 1 lb (450 g) chicken pieces (or beef slices)
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Method

  1. Prepare the Chicken or Beef:
    • Season the chicken or beef with paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
    • Heat a few tablespoons of oil in a pan over medium heat.
    • Fry the seasoned chicken or beef until golden brown and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  2. Cook the Rice:
    • Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
    • In a pot, add the broth and bring it to a boil. Add the rice, curry powder, thyme, and a pinch of salt.
    • Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed (about 15-20 minutes).
    • Once cooked, fluff the rice and set it aside.
  3. Make the Fried Rice:
    • In a large pan or wok, heat 2-3 tablespoons of oil over medium heat.
    • Add the chopped onions, carrots, green beans, and bell peppers. Saut√© for 3-5 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
    • Add the green peas and sweet corn, stirring to combine.
    • Add the cooked rice to the vegetables in the pan and mix everything well.
    • Season with additional salt and pepper to taste, and cook for another 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Serve: Serve the fried rice alongside the fried chicken or beef, with additional vegetables if desired.
