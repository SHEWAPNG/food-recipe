Pineapple

Nutritional Facts

Calories: 82 per cup
Vitamin C: 131% of RDI
Fiber: 2.3g
Manganese: 76% of RDI

How to Eat

Pineapple can be eaten fresh, grilled, or added to salads, desserts, and savory dishes. Slice and cut as desired.

Washing Tips

Rinse the pineapple under running water to remove dirt before cutting.
