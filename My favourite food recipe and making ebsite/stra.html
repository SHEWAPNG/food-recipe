Strawberry

Nutritional Facts

Calories: 49 per cup
Vitamin C: 149% of RDI
Fiber: 3g
Manganese: 29% of RDI

How to Eat

Strawberries are eaten fresh or added to cereals, smoothies, salads, or desserts. Slice as needed for recipes.

Washing Tips

Wash strawberries right before eating under cold running water; avoid soaking to prevent mushiness.
